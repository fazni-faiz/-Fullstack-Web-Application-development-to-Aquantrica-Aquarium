About Us

Aquantrica Aquarium Shop is a family-owned business that has been providing aquarium products and services since 1995. We have a team of experienced and knowledgeable staff who are passionate about aquatics and are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service and quality products.

Our mission is to help you create and maintain a beautiful and healthy aquatic environment. We offer a wide range of products and services, including aquarium design and installation, maintenance and cleaning, and water testing and analysis. We also offer educational resources and workshops to help you learn more about aquatics and how to care for your aquarium.
